Riverborn HEMA Club

WDD 231 | Project Site Plan

Site Name

Riverborn HEMA Club - This name is a play on the name "Tagalog", an ethnic group native to the Pasig River area in the Philippines. Tagalog translates to "people ffrom the river," but can literally interpreted as "riverborn."

Site Purpose

The site acts as an information hub for the Riverborn HEMA club, including an introduction to the art of HEMA, schedules, equipment lists, and how to join. It serves both members and newcomers who are interested in historical martial arts.

Scenarios

  • What kind of gear do I need to begin training?
  • When and where are the club's classes held?
  • How do I sign up or try a class?

Color Schema

Dark Steel (#1f1f1f): Used for headers, backgrounds, and accent elements.
Gold (#d4af37): Used for buttons, highlights, borders, and links.

Typography

Cinzel: Used for all h1 and h2 headings for a historic, formal look.
Roboto: Used for body text and paragraph content for readability.
